Materials Needed:

AT&T Wireless Vinyl Logo (part # 797553334637)
Rubbing Alcohol
Paper Towels
Masking Tape
Plastic Spray Bottle
Ivory Liquid Dish Soap
Credit Card 		Guidelines for AT&T Wireless Logo:

Only install one AT&T Wireless logo per door window, just above the store hours sign. If you location has side by side double doors entering into your sales floor, place the store hours sign on one door window and the logo on the other.

NOTE: If your location has heavily tinted glass, please contact your Visual Merchandising Manager to order a special vinyl that allows for application of the logo to the outside of your door window.

Carefully read through the step by step instructions before you affix the vinyl logo to the glass of your front door. For best results, have two people perform the installation and utilize the materials listed below. If you feel it is necessary, you may contact a local sign vendor to perform the installation, however please make sure they follow these same instructions.

Step by Step Installation:

1. The vinyl logo needs to be installed to your front door, just above your Store Hours sign.

2. You must take down your Store Hours sign and thoroughly clean glass surface with rubbing alcohol and paper towels before you begin the installation.

3. Place the logo at eye level on the inside of the door. Make sure that the logo is positioned so "AT&T Wireless" can be read looking in from the street. Run masking tape across the top and attach the paper to the door. The paper will now look like a flap.

4. Fill the plastic spray bottle with water. Add 1-2 drops of liquid dish soap and shake bottle well to mix the solution. Lift the paper from the bottom and spray the glass surface underneath with solution. Glass should be wet, but avoid getting the masking tape wet.

5. Slowly remove the paper closest to the window to expose the sticky side of the vinyl. Lay the vinyl on the glass.
